# Orion privacy notice template

**Last updated:** replace this date before publishing.

Orion uses privacy-respecting analytics to understand how visitors discover and use the Orion Gold Scalper landing page. We collect an anonymous visitor ID, session ID, page activity, device category, browser family, coarse country/city headers, referrer and campaign parameters such as UTM values and `fbclid`. We do not use browser fingerprinting and do not collect a name, email address or Telegram username unless you voluntarily submit one.

Where required by law, analytics remains disabled until consent. You can decline or withdraw consent through the site's consent controls. Meta Pixel and Meta Conversions API may receive event data such as PageView, ViewContent, Lead and Contact, with browser/server `event_id` values used for deduplication. We do not send the Meta access token to the browser.

Analytics data is retained for the configured retention period, then deleted. You may ask for deletion or access by contacting the published Orion support address. Replace this template with your legal entity, lawful basis, data processor list, international-transfer language and jurisdiction-specific rights before publishing.
